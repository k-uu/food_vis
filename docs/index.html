Visualizing Greenhouse Farming in Finland

*For each crop:

  • Productivity is defined as the yield (kg) divided by the cultivation area (\(m^2\))
  • Variability is calculated as the standard deviation of the yearly fractional change in the measurement.
  • Growth is estimated using the fitted slope from a log-linear regression model. For example, a growth rate of 5% would lead to a \((1.05)^{10} = 1.63\) times increase over 10 years.

Data is accessed from Natural Resources Institute Finland for the period 2014-2025.
